## System-level view

- **L1 (exact repeat):** 100% precision and recall by construction (SHA-256 of the canonical prompt). Every exact repeat is a guaranteed hit; L2 only adds paraphrase catches.
- **L2 (semantic):** Pipeline 2 above — zero false hits is the safety contract.

## Honesty notes

- Curated adversarial set, n=64; Wilson CIs quantify small-n uncertainty. A real-traffic eval (logged Claude Code prompts) is the next step for a tighter bound.
- The guard's antonym lexicon is curated (long-tail/overfit risk); negation and direction-swap rules are general. NLI contradiction detection is the heavier, more general alternative — ruled in/out by a held-out set.
- Single-turn framing: multi-turn prompts carry more shared context and behave differently.
